  1. Find Lunch Spots 

OKAY so there's between 15k-20k of us trekking in the tiny city of St. Louis therefore, restaurants will be super SUPER SUPER packed. Start pinning out locations you want to go for lunch. You will literally have to run to those places right as the session ends in order to get a table and not have to wait 1 hr. for a table + 1 hr+ for your food.

  1. Check Out Seminars and Exhibitors 

MAKE a list of seminars you want to attend. There are SOOOO many of them and the afternoons will be when they are held. Make a list, check it twice, and check the locations of these so you know where they'll be. A number of them will be held offsite at different hotels. [So star them on Google Maps]

LINK TO SEMINARS: https://urbana.org/seminars-tracks

EXHIBITIONS are GREAT. there are so many organizations that will be there with booths showing you missional opportunities in all kinds of fields. Whether it be business, science, technology, engineering, humanitarian, short term missions / long term missions opportunities, educational opportunities...these exhibitors will be in the exhibition hall and it's a great place to explore where God may take you.

while @ URBANA

THE SCHEDULE can be found here: https://urbana.org/sites/urbana/files/Urbana15_schedule_public_940.png

LEARNING GOD’S STORY:morning devos

the devo’s happen every morning during this time. they happen in the convention center and you may pick any room. there, you will read through God’s word and study it alongside the manuscript that you will be handed at check-in. if you wish, you can also study this with your roommates or random people you find but, I encourage you to go attend at least one session that is facilitated.

~IF, you are part of a TRACK - you will have your own location~

THE SESSIONS:awesome speakers, and worship

these sessions happen in the Edward Jones Dome -- people usually try to get in super early to get floor seats. whether you want to get floor seats or not, I encourage you to go early and rest up before praising God and hearing His awesome word.

SEMINARS:way too many awesome workshops

these were mentioned above. plan out which ones you want to attend RIGHT NOW and make sure they are the ones YOU WANT TO ATTEND (DON’T FALL FOR YOUR FRIENDS’ INFLUENCE) there are way too many seminars that are super awesome and help you explore missions and faith in many contexts. some seminars are recorded and you may access them later but there will be some that aren’t -- so start finding seminars and figuring out their locations!

~IF, you are part of a TRACK - you will have your own seminars~

PRAYER:one can never pray or be prayed for enough 1:00-6:30 PM

during the afternoon, there will be prayer rooms where you can pray and receive prayer. there are three rooms:

  • MISSIONAL (2nd floor atrium)
  • WORSHIP AND REFLECTION (America’s Center 124-125)
  • PRAYER MINISTRY (America’s 130

find out what each one is about here: https://urbana.org/blog/prayer-urbana-15

ROOMMATE HUDDLES:time with the roommies-unwind,reflect,pray

this time is probably one of the most important times of the day. it’s right before you kick back in bed and fall asleep. your roommates are here, at URBANA, with you for the same ride. during this time, close out your thoughts for the day by:

  • JOURNALING any closing thoughts from the long day
  • SHARE with one another about your seminar’s (by going to different seminars, you’ll be able to share with one another)
  • PRAY for one another, BE VULNERABLE, BE OPEN, BE HONEST, petitions all your requests to each other and pray for one another.
  • pray for your friends and other attendees @ URBANA
  • pray for you friends and family who are at home
  • pray for God’s message to be heard and engraved into your heart
  • pray for God’s work to be done through you
  • pray for requests on this list [& add your own]: http://bit.ly/urbs15praiseprayer
  • UNWIND 
  • call your family! (although it’ll be late because of time difference) and tell them how you’re doing and check up on them
  • play games!
  • watch movies!
  • talk about life

BUT MOST IMPORTANTLY, GET REST! LONG DAYS ARE AHEAD! DON’T GET SICK AND DON’T MISS OUT
